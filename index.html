Hey, I'm Shubham 👋

A Software developer with a knack in Design Thinking.

I am a full time software devloper, part time hacker and a minimalist. I enjoy writing clean code, I believe in the philosophy, "A Idiot admires complexity, a Genius admires simplicity."

Outside of programming, I enjoy writing my blog, taking long walks, learning about newer innovations.

I've recently adopted the hobby of breaking down tech and teaching them in fun ways to my social group, I really admire the concept and execution of open-source, thus linux became my all time favourite.

I believe hacking is the finest art in programming, it liberates ones dependance on society, and allows for more creative and free work, without the boundation of paywalls, and all the targeted media. It empowers me to create my own solutions, to innovate and experiment without the fear of repercussions or the burden of conformity. It has became a art of innovating instead of stealing as it's usually shown.
