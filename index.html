Hey, I'm Shubham 👋

A Software developer, with a knack in Design Thinking.

I am a full time software developer, part time hacker. I believe in the philosophy, "An idiot admires complexity, a genius admires simplicity." Outside of programming, I enjoy writing my blog, spending time offline and learning about newer innovations.

I've recently adopted the hobby of breaking down tech and teaching them in fun ways to my social group. I really admire the initiative of free open source software (FOSS), thus Linux became my all-time favorite.

I believe hacking is the finest art in programming. It liberates one's dependence on society and allows for more creative and free work, without the boundation of paywalls and firewalls. It empowers one to create their own solutions, to innovate and experiment without the fear of repercussions or the burden of conformity. It is an art of innovating instead of stealing, as usually shown.
